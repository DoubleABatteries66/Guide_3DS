### Wymagana Lektura

Before proceeding, ensure you have read all of the information on [ntrboot](ntrboot)

This method requires temporary access to a Nintendo DSi that is compatible with your flashcart. This method uses the flashcart to run the ntrboot flasher `.nds` file on your NDS. This means that your flashcart must support launching `.nds` files on your NDS's version. Zobacz tabelę flashcart na [ntrboot](ntrboot), aby uzyskać więcej informacji.

Note that in some rare circumstances, it may be possible for the flashing process to **brick** a counterfeit flashcart and render it permanently unusable. This is unlikely, but nevertheless only original listed flashcarts are supported. To reduce the chance of receiving a counterfeit card, it is recommended that you use a reputable site to buy your flashcart (such as [NDS Card](http://www.nds-card.com/))
{: .notice--danger}

### What You Need

* Twojego kompatybilnego z ntrboot flashcarta DS / DSi
* Two devices 
  + **The source DSi**: the Nintendo DSi which is compatible with your flashcart
  + **The target 3DS**: the 3DS family device on stock firmware
* Najnowszej wersji [ds_ntrboot_flasher](https://github.com/ntrteam/ds_ntrboot_flasher/releases/latest){:target="_blank"} *(wersję `dsi`; nie wersję zwykłą)*

### Instrukcje

#### Sekcja I - Przygotowanie

1. Power off **the source DSi**
1. Insert your flashcart's SD card into your computer
1. Umieść plik `ds_ntrboot_flasher_dsi.nds` na karcie SD twojego flashcarta
1. Reinsert your flashcart's SD card back into your flashcart
1. Insert your ntrboot compatible DS / DSi flashcart into **the source DSi**

#### Sekcja II - Wgrywanie ntrboot

1. Launch `ds_ntrboot_flasher_dsi.nds` on **the source DSi** using your flashcart
1. Naciśnij (A), aby kontynuować
1. Użyj przycisków (Góra) i (Dół) na D-Padzie aby wybrać model swojego flashcarta
1. Naciśnij (A), aby kontynuować
1. Press (A) to "inject ntrboothax"
1. Press (A) to select "RETAIL"
1. Naciśnij (A), aby kontynuować
1. Wybierz "EXIT"
